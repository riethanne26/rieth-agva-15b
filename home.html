Welcome to Youth Group

We exist to glorify God by helping students take the next step in their relationship with Christ. G180 meets on Wednesday nights during the months of September through May. Each week includes plenty of hanging out, food, worship, prayer, Bible lessons, and a time for small groups that we call Life Groups. Our goal for G180 is to meet students where they are, encourage students in their faith, help students grow in their understanding of Biblical worldview, provide a safe place for students' spiritual and social growth, and all students to find a ministry home.
